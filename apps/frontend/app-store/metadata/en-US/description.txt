Eos is a minimal todo app. No streaks, no badges, no AI suggesting what you should care about — just a clean place to put what you have to do.

WRITE IN PLAIN TEXT, GET CHIPS
Type "Pick up dry cleaning at 6pm for 20min" and Eos parses the time and duration into chips you can edit later. Add @project to group it, and the project chip shows up too. Edit the text, the chips follow.

SYNCS WITH TELEGRAM
Open Telegram, send a message to the Eos bot, and the task appears in your inbox on iPhone, iPad, and any other device you're signed in on. Reply to the bot to complete or reschedule. You never lose a thought because you didn't have the app open.

WHAT YOU GET
• Today, scheduled, inbox — three views, no menus to learn.
• Drag tasks between days and sections to reschedule.
• Sections you define: morning/afternoon, by project, by location.
• Sign in with Google or Apple to sync across devices, or use it anonymously on one device.
• iPhone and iPad layouts.

PRIVACY
Your tasks are yours. No third-party trackers and no selling data.
